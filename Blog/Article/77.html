CSS3利用Flexbox属性实现一个DIV里 三张图片两端对齐

2015-05-08    Flexbox

HTML

<div class="con">
   <img src="img/s.jpg" alt="">
   <img src="img/s.jpg" alt="">
   <img src="img/s.jpg" alt="">
 </div>

CSS3

.con {
  display: -webkit-flex;
  display: flex;
  -webkit-flex-direction: row;
  flex-direction: row;
  -webkit-flex-wrap: nowrap;
  flex-wrap: nowrap;
  -webkit-justify-content: space-between;
  justify-content: space-between;
}
